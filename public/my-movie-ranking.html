Ajith's Favorite Movie Reviews

My top 3 Marvel movies of all time.

1. Avengers Endgame.

After over a decade of build-up, this film encapsulates all of the best elements of the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

2. Guardians of the Galaxy: Volume 3.

Similar to Endgame, there has been 10 years of buildup with some really lovable characters and deep diving into the past and their origins.

3. Captain America: Civil War

Taking a fan favorite team, the avengers, and having them divide and fight amongst one another is one of the greatest moments in the MCU.
